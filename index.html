Home Rehearsal Tool ☰

Click a Character to Highlight Their Lines

This single-page application is designed to help stage actors rehearse. By clicking on a character’s name, only their lines will be highlighted, making it easier to focus on their part. This demonstrates dynamic DOM manipulation using plain JavaScript without reloading or using third-party libraries.
